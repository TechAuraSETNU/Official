About Us

TechAura

Techaura is a national-level technical festival held in Nagaland, organized by final-year students of the School of Engineering & Technology. The festival aims to spread an atmosphere of technology to society, tapping into the potential and ideas of individuals through various technical competitions, presentations, exhibitions, and seminars. Techaura is not just a celebration of life, culture, and technology but a platform to inspire and encourage students to become smart, sensible leaders and entrepreneurs with great visionaries. The festival recognizes that the world is constantly evolving, and with advancements in high-speed transportation, pervasive internet, and industries, the need for smart and visionary leaders has never been more crucial. Techaura strives to impact the world by inspiring individuals to pursue their dreams, not just for themselves but for the betterment of society as a whole. Through rock bands, cultural shows, dance, and music, Techaura creates an environment hat is both celebratory and thought-provoking, encouraging individuals to reflect on the impact of technology on their lives and to contemplate the possibilities of the future. We encourage everyone to participate in this celebration and join us in creating a spirit of visionary thinking that will help shape the future of our world.
